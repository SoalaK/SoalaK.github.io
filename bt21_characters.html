RJ

Jin's contribution RJ is an alpaca who often wears a parka, because he gets cold easily. Jin was drawing cute alpaca doodles for years before this collaboration, so he was excited to finally bring one to life. The other members also say he looks like an alpaca, especially when he eats.

RJ was taken away by TATA to join BT21. They were an alter-ego, DJ. RJ loves eating, and can commit to gluttony, especially when it comes to eating bread. Even though they originated from Machu Picchu and had to donate their fur back then, they hate shaving.


Shooky

SHOOKY is a light brown shape cookie-alike with brown arms and legs. They have a left thick and a right thin eyebrow, and a brown inner mouth with bucktooth.

SHOOKY is created by a baker known as MUSTASHU. They came to life and joined BT21. SHOOKY is naughty and likes making jokes with the other Universtar members.


Mang

J-Hope's character Mang is a dancing pony, but he keeps his face hidden by wearing a mask. His nose is heart-shaped, because J-Hope's lips form a heart when he laughs. There are several meanings to 'mang' in Korean. It can translate as 'a pony', 'to fail', and it's the latter part of the word 'hope' (huimang).

MANG is a purple creature-alike wearing a light-blue horse mask. Their horse mask has a pink heart-shape nose, an indigo hair, and long string with a short indigo tail attached to the bottom of the hair. Mang is evidently identified as non-binary or gender neutral as they had chosen to go in between the male and female toilet doors during the BT21 discussion video. Mang can also be genderfluid, as they shift between the pronouns 'she' and 'he' in various descriptions.


Koya

Koya is a sleepy blue koala. He has removable ears, and a purple nose as V once made a comment that purple represents love. RM drew pages and pages of koalas in the initial sketching stage, as he thinks koalas are really cute.

KOYA is always sleeping but is actually a good thinker. When shocked, KOYA wakes up, and their ears fall off! KOYA formerly lived in the forest with their friends TOLL, LEAF, and BAEBY, while facing MOYA before getting taken away by TATA.


Chimmy

Chimmy is a playful puppy in yellow represents that BTS' overall image. BTS reminds people of puppies since the boys love to hang out and are super active. That's why Jimin created a character that's a puppy.

CHIMMY always sports the signature yellow hoodie and is compelled to work hard on anything that catches CHIMMY’s attention. Jimin also thinks Chimmy’s chubby cheeks remind people of his face when BTS first debuted.


Tata

Tata, a huge heart-headed character, is an alien with transforming powers. His limbs can bend and stretch into weird lengths and shapes. He's a character that V has been drawing for some time, and he wanted the character to be more unique than cute, as he personally loves unique characters. bt21_official.

TATA is from outer space that can perform love with magic. When TATA gets fed up with magic and instead wishes to put effort to show love, they runs away to Earth with VAN. TATA is responsible for the creation of BT21.


Cooky

Cooky, created by Jungkook, is a rabbit with attitude. He works out a lot and imagines that he transforms into a huge muscle man when he gets angry. Jungkook is known for his endless gym workouts to maintain his six-pack, so in his initial sketches, he drew a rabbit with a six-pack.

COOKY is a pink rabbit with the right ear bending down, the left triangle eyebrow is thick while the right normal one is thin. The inner ears, hands and heart-shape on the back of the tail is white.


Van

VAN is a robot created by TATA to spread love on Earth.

VAN is a humanoid robot with a pointy cone on its head. It has two different colors on both sides of its body, which splits them to half: it is gray on the left-side and white on the right-side, and the eye shows a "X" in the left-side and "O" in the right-side.
